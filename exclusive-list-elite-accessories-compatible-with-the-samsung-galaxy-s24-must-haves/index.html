What to Look for in Galaxy S24 Accessories

The only accessory pieces you’re getting with the Galaxy S24 are a USB-C cable and a SIM cover ejection pin, meaning you’ll have to at least get a charger if you already don’t have one.

When shopping for a charger, unless you buy an official charger from Samsung, make sure the charger you end up with supports the Programmable Power Supply (PPS) feature. PPS is an optional part of the USB PD protocol , and it allows the charger to supply variable voltage and amperage to the device it’s charging.

And since Samsung devices feature unique USB PD profiles not supported by the majority of third-party chargers, most third-party chargers that support USB PD but do not come with PPS will deliver only up to 18W of power to your phone instead of 25W, the max charging power the Galaxy S24 supports.

Next, when it comes to USB-C cables , you’ll be alright with a USB-C charging cable that tops out at 3 amps since the Galaxy S24’s 25W fast charging profile stays below 3A. If you need a cable for multiple devices, I recommend getting a 6A cable.

Earbuds-wise, Galaxy S devices work best with Galaxy Buds. All current Galaxy Buds flavors—the Galaxy Buds2 , the Galaxy Buds2 Pro , and the Galaxy Buds FE —have pretty good sound and respectable battery life, making them solid choices for every Android phone owner out there, not only Samsung Galaxy phone owners.

As for the wireless charger, ensure you get one that supports Galaxy phones and tops out at 10W or more. Note that only a few wireless chargers can deliver 15W to Galaxy phones, the maximum amount of power supported by Galaxy phones, and they’re all quite pricey.

Next, a 10,000mAh power bank should be enough for most, and you should get almost two full charges from it. Regarding screen protectors, I advise everyone to get one that comes with an installation kit because installing a screen protector without one can be a pain.

Okay, now that we’ve covered all that, let me show you the best accessories for your Samsung Galaxy S24.

Best Earbuds for the Galaxy S24: Samsung Galaxy Buds2 Pro

Samsung

The Samsung Galaxy Buds 2 Pro are high-end wireless earbuds and the best earbuds for your Galaxy S24. They offer crisp, balanced sound, respectable battery life, excellent ANC, and IPX7 water resistance. When used with a Samsung phone, you also get features such as 360 Audio for surround sound and Wireless PowerShare, allowing you to charge the earbuds by placing them on the back of your S24.

Regarding the sound profile, the Galaxy Buds 2 Pro offers a balanced sound with a slight boost to lower frequencies, which results in a bit of extra bass that doesn’t take over and that sounds great with most genres of music, including hip-hop, electronic, and guitar-driven genres.

The design here is just right. Unlike the original Buds Pro, the 2nd generation features a slightly rubberized, matte finish that looks and feels much better than the first generation’s glossy finish. The case is also matte, rubberized, and super grippy.

When it comes to negatives, the buds don’t have the best fit and, in most cases, you’ll need to occasionally adjust them, especially while wearing them when walking or doing something else that includes lots of body movement. Also, the Galaxy Wearable app has different EQ presets but doesn’t support custom EQ adjustments.

Check out the Galaxy Buds 2 or the Galaxy Buds FE if you want something more budget-friendly. Both models offer usable ANC and pretty solid battery life. The most significant difference between the two is that the Galaxy Buds 2 offer a more balanced sound, while the Galaxy Buds FE have lots of extra bass—some would even call them too bassy.

Do I need Galaxy Buds for my Galaxy S24?

Absolutely not. While different Galaxy Buds models work great when paired with the Samsung Galaxy S24, you can use any pair of Bluetooth earbuds with your phone. You can also use wired models; just make sure to get a USB-C-to-3.5mm adapter.

Will any type of phone case work for my Galaxy S24?

No, it won’t. For example, while the Galaxy S23 and the Galaxy S24 look almost the same, the new model is slightly taller and narrower, meaning cases made for the Galaxy S23 won’t fit. In other words, only use cases specifically designed for the Galaxy S24.

What wattage do my chargers need to be at for my Galaxy S24?

In theory, any phone charger with a USB-C power connector will do, no matter its wattage. The only downside is that there’s a good chance you won’t get the full 25W charging power supported by the Galaxy S24.

Your best bet is to get the official 25W charger from Samsung or buy a 25W or a more powerful wall charger with support for Programmable Power Supply (PPS).

Thanks to Samsung’s unusual charging profiles, only third-party chargers with USB PD and PPS —PPS allows for variable voltage and amperage negotiation between the charger and the device its charging— can deliver 25W to your phone as long as their maximum charging rate is set at 25W or more.

Does the S24 come with a wall charger?

No, it doesn’t. You’re only getting a USB-C cable and a SIM ejection pin.

Does the S24 have a headphone jack?

No, it doesn’t. The Samsung Galaxy S24 only features a single USB-C port.
