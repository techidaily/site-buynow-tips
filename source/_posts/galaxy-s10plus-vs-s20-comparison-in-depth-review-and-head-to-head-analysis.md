## **Gameplay: A joy that wanes with time**

 Fighting starts out feeling really cool. In the early levels there‚Äôs a definite sense of being a superhero when beating up henchmen and robots. Initially, the heroes feel like they have unique methods of navigation and combat, but the longer you play the more you begin to realize that all the characters play pretty much the same. ## **Customization: Compromised by monetization**

Unfortunately, it doesn‚Äôt alter your character's appearance and only provides incremental stat improvements, so it ends up feeling pretty meaningless.
